Veg Starters

Baby Corn 65

Ingredients: Baby Corn, Cornflour, Rice Flour, Curd, Red Chili Powder, Garlic, Curry Leaves

Process: Parboil baby corn, marinate with curd and spices, coat with cornflour and rice flour, deep fry until crispy, toss with fried curry leaves and garlic, serve hot.

Paneer Tikka

Ingredients: Paneer, Yogurt, Spices, Capsicum, Onion, Skewers

Process: Marinate paneer cubes with yogurt and spices, thread onto skewers with capsicum and onion, grill or bake until golden, and serve with mint chutney.

Paneer 65

Ingredients: Paneer, Cornflour, Curd, Red Chili Powder, Garlic, Curry Leaves

Process: Marinate paneer with spices and curd, coat with cornflour, deep fry until golden, toss with curry leaves and garlic, serve hot.

Stuffed Mushrooms

Ingredients: Mushrooms, Cheese, Garlic, Herbs, Breadcrumbs

Process: Remove mushroom stems, mix cheese, garlic, and herbs for stuffing, fill mushrooms, top with breadcrumbs, bake until golden, and serve hot.

Pav Bhaji

Ingredients: Pav Buns, Potatoes, Tomatoes, Onions, Peas, Capsicum, Butter, Pav Bhaji Masala

Process: Boil and mash vegetables. Saut√© onions, tomatoes, and capsicum in butter. Add mashed vegetables and pav bhaji masala, cook until well blended. Toast pav buns with butter and serve hot with bhaji, onion, and lemon.

Mirchi Bajji

Ingredients: Green Chilies, Besan (Gram Flour), Rice Flour, Carom Seeds, Salt, Oil

Process: Slit green chilies and deseed if needed. Prepare batter using besan, rice flour, spices. Dip chilies in batter and deep fry until golden. Optionally, cut and refry for "cut mirchi" style. Serve hot with chutney.

Pani Puri

Ingredients: Puri, Boiled Potatoes, Chickpeas, Tamarind Chutney, Mint Water, Spices

Process: Fill puris with mashed potatoes and chickpeas, add tamarind chutney, pour chilled mint water, and serve immediately for a burst of flavors.

Non-Veg Starters

Chicken Tikka

Ingredients: Chicken, Yogurt, Spices, Lemon, Skewers

Process: Marinate chicken with yogurt, spices, and lemon juice, thread onto skewers, grill or bake until charred, and serve with mint chutney.

Prawn Tempura

Ingredients: Prawns, Tempura Batter, Salt, Pepper, Oil

Process: Dip prawns in tempura batter, deep fry until crispy, drain excess oil, and serve hot with a dipping sauce.

Spicy Chicken Wings

Ingredients: Chicken Wings, Hot Sauce, Butter, Garlic, Pepper

Process: Marinate chicken wings with spices, bake or fry until crispy, toss in hot sauce and butter mix, and serve hot.

Mutton Seekh Kebab

Ingredients: Minced Mutton, Spices, Garlic, Ginger, Skewers

Process: Mix minced mutton with spices, garlic, and ginger, shape onto skewers, grill until cooked, and serve with onion rings and lemon wedges.

Fish Fingers

Ingredients: Fish Fillets, Breadcrumbs, Egg, Flour, Spices

Process: Coat fish fillets with flour, dip in beaten egg, coat with breadcrumbs, deep fry until golden brown, and serve with tartar sauce.

Tandoori Chicken

Ingredients: Chicken, Curd, Red Chili Powder, Turmeric, Garam Masala, Ginger Garlic Paste, Lemon Juice, Oil

Process: Marinate chicken with curd, spices, ginger garlic paste, and lemon juice. Let it rest for a few hours. Grill or bake until charred and fully cooked. Brush with oil and serve hot with onion rings and lemon wedges.

Chicken Malai Tikka

Ingredients: Boneless Chicken, Fresh Cream, Curd, Ginger Garlic Paste, Green Chili Paste, Grated Cheese, Cardamom Powder, Salt

Process: Marinate chicken in a creamy mixture of curd, cream, cheese, and spices. Let it rest for a few hours. Skewer and grill until tender and slightly charred. Serve hot with mint chutney.

Chicken Lollipop

Ingredients: Chicken Wings (Lollipop Cut), Ginger Garlic Paste, Red Chili Sauce, Soy Sauce, Cornflour, All-purpose Flour, Salt

Process: Marinate chicken wings with sauces and spices, coat in a flour mixture, and deep fry until crispy. Serve hot with spicy sauce or schezwan dip.

Prawns 65

Ingredients: Prawns, Cornflour, Rice Flour, Curd, Red Chili Powder, Garlic, Curry Leaves

Process: Marinate prawns with curd and spices, coat with cornflour and rice flour, deep fry until crispy. Toss with fried garlic and curry leaves. Serve hot.

Tandoori Fish

Ingredients: Fish Fillets, Curd, Red Chili Powder, Turmeric, Garam Masala, Lemon Juice, Ginger Garlic Paste, Oil

Process: Marinate fish with curd, spices, and lemon juice. Rest for at least 1 hour. Grill or bake until nicely charred and cooked through. Brush with oil and serve hot with chutney and lemon.

Veg Biryanis

Vegetable Biryani (South Indian Style)

Ingredients: Basmati Rice, Mixed Vegetables, Curd, Mint, Green Chilies, Garam Masala

Process: Saut√© vegetables with spices and curd. Layer with half-cooked rice, dum cook until aromatic. Garnish with mint and fried onions.

Mushroom Biryani

Ingredients: Mushrooms, Basmati Rice, Onions, Green Chilies, Biryani Masala, Mint

Process: Cook mushrooms with onions and spices. Layer with rice and steam until flavors infuse. Serve hot with raita.

Paneer Biryani

Ingredients: Paneer Cubes, Basmati Rice, Curd, Whole Spices, Saffron, Mint

Process: Marinate paneer in spiced curd, cook gently, then layer with rice and saffron. Dum cook and serve with salad.

Brinjal (Kathirikai) Biryani

Ingredients: Brinjal, Seeraga Samba Rice, Tamarind, Red Chili Powder, Mustard Seeds, Curry Leaves

Process: Cook brinjal in a spicy tamarind masala, add rice, and simmer until flavors combine. Perfect with raita or chips.

Raw Jackfruit (Kathal) Biryani

Ingredients: Raw Jackfruit, Basmati Rice, Curd, Ginger Garlic Paste, Whole Spices, Mint

Process: Boil jackfruit, marinate in curd masala, then layer with rice. Dum cook for a meaty veg delight.

Cauliflower (Gobi) Biryani

Ingredients: Cauliflower Florets, Basmati Rice, Curd, Green Chilies, Spices, Curry Leaves

Process: Blanch and fry cauliflower, cook with masala and rice, layer and steam until fluffy. Serve hot with curd.

Tomato Biryani

Ingredients: Ripe Tomatoes, Basmati Rice, Mustard Seeds, Green Chilies, Garlic, Curry Leaves

Process: Cook tomatoes with mustard and curry leaves until pulpy. Add rice and water, pressure cook for a tangy biryani experience.

Non - Veg Biryanis

Fish Biryani (Mangalorean/Kerala Style)

Ingredients: Fish Fillets, Basmati Rice, Coconut Milk, Curry Leaves, Green Chilies, Spices

Process: Fry fish lightly. Cook rice with coconut milk and spices. Layer with fish pieces and gently steam. Serve with lemon and raita.

Nalli Gosh Biryani

Ingredients: Nalli Mutton (Mutton Shank), Basmati Rice, Curd, Fried Onions, Saffron, Whole Spices

Process: Marinate mutton shanks with curd and spices overnight. Slow-cook the meat until tender. Layer with basmati rice, saffron, and fried onions. Dum cook for rich, aromatic flavor. Serve with raita or salan.

Chicken Fry Piece Biryani

Ingredients: Fried Chicken Pieces, Basmati Rice, Curd, Onions, Garam Masala, Mint

Process: Marinate and fry chicken. Layer with rice and spiced gravy. Steam to combine flavors. Enjoy with boiled egg and onion salad.

Tandoori Chicken Biryani

Ingredients: Tandoori Chicken, Basmati Rice, Curd, Fried Onions, Mint, Saffron, Whole Spices

Process: Marinate chicken with tandoori spices and grill until smoky. Cook basmati rice with whole spices. Layer rice with grilled chicken, fried onions, saffron, and mint. Dum cook for a smoky and spicy biryani experience.

Chicken Lollipop Biryani

Ingredients: Chicken Lollipop, Basmati Rice, Curd, Green Chilies, Spices, Mint

Process: Fry marinated lollipop pieces, prepare biryani rice with masala, and layer with chicken. Dum cook and serve spicy!

Mutton Fry Piece Biryani

Ingredients: Mutton, Basmati Rice, Ginger Garlic Paste, Red Chili Powder, Garam Masala, Mint, Oil

Process: Pressure cook and fry mutton pieces. Cook rice separately. Layer and steam fry mutton with rice. Serve hot with raita.

Mutton Dum Biryani

Ingredients: Mutton, Curd, Basmati Rice, Fried Onions, Saffron, Mint, Ghee

Process: Marinate mutton overnight. Layer with rice, fried onions, and saffron milk. Dum cook until mutton is tender and rice is aromatic.

Prawn Fry Biryani

Ingredients: Prawns, Basmati Rice, Coconut Milk, Green Masala, Curry Leaves, Garam Masala

Process: Marinate and fry prawns. Cook rice with mild spices and coconut milk. Mix and steam with fried prawns on top. Serve coastal-style!

Hyderabadi Chicken Dum Biryani

Ingredients: Chicken, Basmati Rice, Curd, Fried Onions, Mint, Saffron, Whole Spices

Process: Marinate chicken in curd and spices. Layer with half-cooked rice, mint, and fried onions. Dum cook until flavors infuse deeply.

Egg Masala Biryani

Ingredients: Boiled Eggs, Basmati Rice, Onion-Tomato Masala, Green Chilies, Spices

Process: Fry boiled eggs in masala. Cook rice and layer with spicy egg gravy. Dum cook and garnish with coriander leaves.

Mixed Fry Piece Biryani

Ingredients: Fried Chicken, Mutton, Prawns, Basmati Rice, Spices, Curd, Fried Onions

Process: Fry all meats separately with spices. Layer with rice, fried onions, and masala. Dum cook until fully blended. A royal biryani feast!

Juices

Watermelon Juice

Ingredients: Watermelon, Lemon, Mint

Process: Blend watermelon, add lemon juice, garnish with mint, serve chilled.

Muskmelon Juice

Ingredients: Muskmelon, Sugar, Water, Ice Cubes

Process: Blend muskmelon with sugar and water, strain if needed, serve chilled with ice cubes.

Cashew Juice

Ingredients: Cashew Fruit, Sugar, Water, Lemon Juice

Process: Blend cashew fruit with water, strain, add sugar and lemon juice, mix well, serve chilled.

Orange Juice

Ingredients: Fresh Oranges, Sugar, Ice Cubes, Salt (optional)

Process: Squeeze fresh oranges or blend and strain. Add sugar and a pinch of salt if needed. Serve chilled with ice.

Mango Juice

Ingredients: Ripe Mangoes, Water, Sugar, Ice Cubes

Process: Blend peeled mango pieces with water and sugar. Strain if needed and serve chilled with ice.

Pineapple Juice

Ingredients: Pineapple Chunks, Water, Sugar, Ice Cubes

Process: Blend pineapple with water and sugar. Strain and serve cold with ice cubes.

Grape Juice

Ingredients: Seedless Grapes, Sugar, Water, Lemon Juice, Ice

Process: Blend grapes with water and sugar. Strain, add a dash of lemon juice, and serve with ice.

Apple Juice

Ingredients: Apples, Water, Sugar or Honey, Lemon Juice

Process: Blend chopped apples with water and lemon juice. Strain and sweeten if needed. Serve chilled.

Mosambi (Sweet Lime) Juice

Ingredients: Sweet Lime (Mosambi), Sugar, Salt, Ice

Process: Extract juice from mosambi. Add sugar and a pinch of salt. Stir well and serve chilled.

Amla (Indian Gooseberry) Juice

Ingredients: Amla (Gooseberries), Water, Honey or Sugar, Ginger, Black Salt

Process: Boil or steam amlas, remove seeds, and blend with water, ginger, and honey. Add a pinch of black salt. Strain and serve chilled.

Guava Juice

Ingredients: Ripe Guavas, Water, Sugar, Lemon Juice, Ice

Process: Blend chopped guavas with water and sugar. Strain to remove seeds. Add lemon juice and serve chilled with ice cubes.
